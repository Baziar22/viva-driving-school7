L About VIVA

North London's trusted route to a full licence

VIVA Driving School GB is a DVSA approved driving school built on one idea: confidence comes from clear instruction, patient coaching, and lessons structured around how you learn — not a one-size-fits-all syllabus.

Every lesson is run by a fully qualified, DVSA approved instructor in a modern, dual-control car. Whether you're starting from zero, brushing up after a break, or fast-tracking towards a test date, your plan is built around your goals, your schedule, and your test centre.
